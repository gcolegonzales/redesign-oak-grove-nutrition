Meet the Club

Local, family-owned, and here for your best day.

Oak Grove Nutrition is a Prairieville nutrition club run by Kelsey Stafford — a friendly, country-style spot where the loaded teas are as colorful as the welcome. We built this place around one idea: good-for-you drinks should actually taste great and make your morning better.

Whether you're grabbing an energizing tea before work, a protein shake instead of skipping breakfast, or a fun refresher for the kids, we've got a cup for it — and we'll remember your order next time.
